About the Author

The journey so far....

Síle Moloney is an MA journalism student at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism at City University of New York. She is a graduate of Galway & Mayo Institute of Technology, University College Dublin and The London School of Journalism. She first left her native Ireland in 1989, and has been roaming the globe ever since..

In returning to study, she finally decided to take the advice of a wise and wonderful German lady she met while volunteering in Ecuador in 2010:

"It's never too late to change your life.....but start today!"
